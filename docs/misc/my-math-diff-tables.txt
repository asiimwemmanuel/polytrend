this is for quartic sequences

2, 7, 17, 35, 61, ...

7 - 2 = 5
17 - 7 = 10 	10 - 5 = 5
35 - 17 = 18	18 - 10 = 8 	8 - 5 = 3
61 - 35 = 26	26 - 18 = 8 	8 - 8 = 0	0 - 3 = -3

Applying the general formula for the above sequence:
	2 + 5(n-1) + 5(n-1)(n-2)/2 + 3(n-1)(n-2)(n-3)/6 - 3(n-1)(n-2)(n-3)(n-4)/24
